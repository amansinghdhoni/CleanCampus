Waste Segregation Encyclopedia

Know your waste. Save the planet.

🟢 Green Bin: Biodegradable (Wet)

What goes here?

🍌 Fruit Peels 🍕 Leftover Food ☕ Coffee Grounds 🥚 Egg Shells 🍂 Garden Leaves 🦴 Meat Bones
Pro-Tip: Do not put wet waste in plastic bags. Empty the waste directly into the bin to help in composting!

Process: Composting

This waste is sent to our campus biomethanation plant to produce organic manure for our gardens.

🔵 Blue Bin: Recyclable (Dry)

What goes here?

🧴 Plastic Bottles 📦 Cardboard Boxes 📰 Newspapers 🥤 Soda Cans 🥛 Milk Packets (Rinsed) 🛍️ Clean Wrappers
Pro-Tip: Rinse milk packets and food containers. Dirty plastic cannot be recycled!

Impact: Recycling

Dry waste is sorted and sold to authorized recyclers, generating revenue for campus green initiatives.

🔴 Red/Black Bin: Hazardous & E-Waste

Hazardous Waste

🔋 Batteries 💡 CFL Bulbs 🩹 Used Bandages 🧴 Expired Medicines 🔌 Broken Chargers

These items contain toxic chemicals. Never mix them with regular waste as they can contaminate soil and water.
